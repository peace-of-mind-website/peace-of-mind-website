Child Mind Institute

The Child Mind Institute's clinical practice is dedicated to providing the highest quality evidence-based care to children and families faced with mental health and learning disorders. The clinicians bring experience, expertise and a team approach to diagnosing and treating your child.

National Institute of Mental Health

The National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH) is the lead federal agency for research on mental disorders. NIMH is one of the 27 Institutes and Centers that make up the National Institutes of Health (NIH), the largest biomedical research agency in the world.

Mental Health America

Mental Health America (MHA) is the nation's leading community-based nonprofit dedicated to addressing the needs of those living with mental illness and to promoting the overall mental health of all Americans. Their current work is guided by the Before Stage 4 philosophy.
